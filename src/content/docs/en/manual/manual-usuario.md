Welcome to the **Monsta User Manual**! This section is designed to help you navigate the interface, understand your environment's metrics, and configure the platform according to your operational workflow.

Whether you are a NOC operator who needs to monitor real-time alerts or a systems administrator setting up new environments, this manual covers all daily interactions with the system.

---

## What will you find in this section?

To make your search easier, the manual is divided into specific topics related to the interface features:

* [**Top Bar**](/en/manual/barra-superior/recursos-disponiveis): Get to know Monsta's top bar, its features and information.
* [**Devices**](/en/manual/dispositivos/pagina-principal): Learn how to view the inventory of monitored assets, analyze the individual status of servers, switches, or routers, and check sensors.
* [**Dashboards**](/en/manual/paineis/paineis): How to interact with Dashboards, customize performance widgets, and consolidate the most important metrics on a single screen.
* [**Timeline**](/en/manual/linha-tempo/linha-do-tempo): Learn how to audit the event history, identify intermittencies, and retroactively analyze your environment's behavior.
* [**Alert Groups**](/en/manual/grupos-alertas/alertas): Understand the organization of notification rules and how alerts are prioritized and distributed to the responsible teams.
* [**Settings**](/en/manual/configuracoes/recursos): Guides on adjusting profile preferences, interface customizations, and basic user account management.
