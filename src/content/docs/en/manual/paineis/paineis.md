The "Dashboards" tab is your command center to quickly and efficiently view the most important indicators of your system. Imagine a car dashboard that displays vital information about the vehicle.

**Create custom dashboards**: Organize your widgets in the way that best meets your needs, grouping related indicators for deeper analysis.  
  
**Add and customize widgets**: Choose from a variety of widgets to display data from different sources, such as line charts, maps, groups, arrows and more. Customize each one with your most relevant data.  
  
**Drag and drop**: Easily arrange your widgets by dragging them to the desired position on the dashboard.

## Create dashboards

![image-1646828376689.png](../../../../../assets/images/p35_image-1646828376689.png)
**New Dashboard**: Adds a new Dashboard

---

![image-1646828800892.png](../../../../../assets/images/p35_image-1646828800892.png)
- **Dashboard Name**: This field should contain the name of the dashboard.
- **Grid Size**: This option allows you to set the grid spacing that will be used as a base for placing widgets.
- **Background Color**: Allows selecting the dashboard background color. When using automatic mode, the dashboard will follow the settings of the [night mode](/en/manual/barra-superior/recursos-disponiveis#tools).

## Rotation Mode

![image-1732651770348.png](../../../../../assets/images/p35_image-1732651770348.png) 
**Rotation mode**: When more than one dashboard is available you can configure Monsta to rotate them automatically. 
![image-1646828601330.png](../../../../../assets/images/p35_image-1646828601330.png)
Using the button, you can configure the time, in minutes, that each dashboard should remain displayed, as well as select which ones should be active for display.

![image-1756123340457.png](../../../../../assets/images/p35_image-1756123340457.png)
**Widget Menu**: Your dashboard is fully customizable thanks to the **Widget Menu** on the left. This menu works as a component library, where you can find and select various types of visualizations for your dashboard. To assemble your dashboard, simply browse the categories in the menu, click on the widget you want and click on the workspace to insert it. The order and positioning of widgets can be adjusted at any time, allowing you to create a dashboard that meets your needs exactly.
