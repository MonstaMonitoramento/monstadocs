---
title: "How does the Trial work?"
---

The Monsta trial version can monitor unlimited devices and monitors. To activate the Monsta trial, visit our downloads page ([https://www.monsta.com.br/downloads](https://www.monsta.com.br/downloads)) and download the installer for the operating system of your choice. Use our [Installation Manual](/en/start/instalacao/instalacao-monsta#instalação) to assist you in this process.

**How to activate the Trial**:

1. **Download Monsta from the site**: [https://www.monsta.com.br/downloads](https://monsta.com.br/)
2. **Install Monsta on your server;** 
     - Fedora/Redhat: `dnf -y install monsta-latest.rpm`  
     - Debian/Ubuntu: `dpkg -i monsta-latest.deb`
3. **Access the web interface;**
4. **Register your information for first access;**
5. **Click on "Activate Trial".**

All features are active except alert sending, which will be available only for subscription plans.

If you wish to convert your trial into a subscription, just click the "**Subscribe**" button located at the top right corner of your screen, sign in with the credentials that were registered in "**Step 4**" above and proceed to the payment screen.
