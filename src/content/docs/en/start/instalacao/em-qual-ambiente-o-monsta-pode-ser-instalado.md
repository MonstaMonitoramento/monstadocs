sidebar:

---

To install Monsta, you will need a Linux-based operating system. It is designed to work well in those environments.

Specifically, Monsta is compatible with popular Linux distributions such as:

- **Ubuntu**: One of the most widely used distributions with strong community support.
- **Debian**: Known for its stability and large software repository.
- **CentOS**: An enterprise-grade, stable distribution based on the Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) source code.
- **Fedora**: A cutting-edge distribution that serves as a testing ground for technologies that may be included in RHEL.
- **Red Hat**: A commercial Linux distribution, known for its stability, security, and long-term support for enterprise environments.

:::tip
Use our [Installation Manual](/en/start/instalacao/instalacao-monsta#instalação) to download and install Monsta in a few minutes in your environment.
:::
