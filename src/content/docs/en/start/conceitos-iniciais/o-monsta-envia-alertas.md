  order: 4
---

Monsta offers a flexible and comprehensive alert system to ensure you are immediately notified of any problem or anomaly detected in your IT infrastructure. For your convenience and to suit your communication preferences, Monsta can send alerts through the following channels:

- **Email**: Receive detailed notifications directly in your email inbox. Email alerts can include complete information about the event, the affected device, the metric that triggered the alert, and the time of occurrence.
- **Telegram**: Use Telegram to receive instant alerts in your preferred messaging app. Telegram offers fast and reliable notifications, allowing you to stay updated even on the go.
- **SMS (Text Message)**: For critical alerts that require immediate attention, Monsta can send notifications via SMS directly to your mobile phone. This option ensures you are informed even without internet access or a messaging app.

These integrations are fully native in Monsta, meaning you don't need to install or configure any additional software. You can configure alert channels individually for different types of events and severity levels, ensuring that important notifications reach you in the most effective way. This flexibility enables your IT team to proactively respond to incidents, minimizing downtime and ensuring the continuity of your services.
