---
title: "How do I configure Telegram to receive Monsta alerts?"
---

Telegram is an instant messaging service widely recognized for its emphasis on **speed** and **security**. It allows users to send text messages, voice and video calls, media and files of any type, operating synchronized across multiple devices simultaneously. Besides one-on-one and group conversations, it stands out for the ability to host public or private **channels** with unlimited audiences and for its open API, which encourages the creation of *bots* and custom tools.

Monsta uses a Telegram bot to send alerts. To join this bot, do the following:

- **In Monsta**, access the **"Alert Groups"** menu and click the group in which you want to receive messages;
- Inside the selected group, go to the **"Monsta Alerts"** tab;
- Select the option **"Monsta: Telegram"** and click to activate it;
- Click the **"Manage registered users"** button;
- Copy the **code** shown on the screen;

To activate alerts, **search Telegram for "MonstaTecnologiaBot"**. When you open the bot chat, **enter the code provided**. If the procedure is successful, you will receive the message "**Alerts Activated**". From that moment on, all users who activated the code will start receiving the alerts specific to that group.
