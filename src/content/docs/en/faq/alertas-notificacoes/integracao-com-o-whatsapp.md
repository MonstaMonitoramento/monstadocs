---
title: "Integration with WhatsApp"
---

Currently, **Monsta** does not have a direct integration for sending and receiving messages through **WhatsApp**.

However, we are working so that, in the near future, Monsta can **natively communicate with the main Official Partner platforms (BSPs)** that integrate the **Meta WhatsApp Business API**. This development will serve other platforms such as Slack and Discord.

- - - 

## 🎯 How Will the Future Integration Work?

Our integration approach aims to provide a robust, scalable solution in compliance with Meta's rules.

Monsta will be adapted to connect to **third-party solution providers (BSPs)** certified by Meta. This ensures that WhatsApp communication within Monsta will use a secure, stable and **official** platform, minimizing blocking risks and ensuring message delivery.

> 📝 **What is a BSP?** A *Business Solution Provider* (BSP) is an official Meta partner company authorized to provide access to and manage the infrastructure of the WhatsApp Business API.
