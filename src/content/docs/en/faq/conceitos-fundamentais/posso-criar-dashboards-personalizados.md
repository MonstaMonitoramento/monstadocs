---
title: "Can I create custom dashboards?"
---

Yes! Monsta offers the powerful capability to create **fully customizable dashboards** through the [Dashboards](/en/manual/paineis/paineis). We understand that every IT environment is unique and that you need to view the information most relevant to your daily work and specific needs.

With Monsta's customizable dashboards, you can:

- **Select the metrics that matter**: Choose from a wide range of monitored data (server performance, network traffic, application status, etc.) to display exactly what you need to track.
- **Organize information your way**: Drag and drop widgets, resize them, and place them in the layout you find most intuitive and efficient for your analysis.
- **Visualize data clearly**: Use different chart types to represent data in the most informative and easy-to-understand way.
- **Filter and segment data**: Focus on specific devices, services, or time ranges for more targeted analysis.
- **Create multiple dashboards**: Build different panels for different teams, projects, or areas of your infrastructure, each focused on the information most relevant to that context.
- **Share dashboards**: Facilitate communication and collaboration by sharing your custom dashboards with other team members or external parties.

The flexibility of Monsta's custom dashboards allows you to create **tailored views** of your IT infrastructure, making it easier to identify trends, detect issues, and make informed decisions. Have complete control over how you view and interact with your monitoring data.
