---
title: "Can I switch my Monsta to another account?"
---

Yes, you can switch the account used by your Monsta; however, alert credits and users registered on Telegram are not migrated and will remain on the old account.

To change your account in Monsta, follow these steps:

- Click the gear icon in the top-right corner;
- Select "About Monsta";
- Select the "License and account" tab;
- Click the "Switch account" button.

The login screen will appear and you can enter the new account's credentials. After logging in, the screen with available licenses will appear. Click the "Select" button for the key you want to use. After that, your Monsta will be connected to your new account on our site.
