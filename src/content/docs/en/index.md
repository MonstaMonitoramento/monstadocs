Managing a computer network without adequate monitoring is like flying a plane without instruments. **Monsta** was created precisely to be your control panel: a complete, agile, and above all, uncomplicated IT infrastructure monitoring solution.

Our mission is to ensure you have full visibility of your environment, from servers and routers to UPS units, fiber optic equipment (OLT/ONU), and IoT devices, detecting problems in real-time before they affect your operations.

This documentation is designed to guide you from installation in less than 1 minute to the most advanced configurations for alerts and custom scripts.

---

## What will you find here?

Our knowledge base is organized so you can find quick and accurate answers:

* 🚀 **Getting Started**: The basics you need to know. Quick guides for installation, getting started, and mapping your network topology.
* 📖 **User Manual**: Detailed instructions on the entire Monsta interface. Learn to navigate screens, create custom dashboards, configure alerts (Email, SMS, Telegram), and manage devices.
* ⚙️ **Technical Documentation**: Dive deeper into collection protocols (SNMP, WMI), creating custom monitors using the **Lua** language, and other integrations.
* ❓ **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**: Quick answers to the most common questions (like the differences between Probe and Agent) and solutions for troubleshooting scenarios.
* 💡 **Extra Guides**: Specific use cases, equipment configuration tutorials and other tools, monitoring best practices, and tips for optimizing your environment's performance.
